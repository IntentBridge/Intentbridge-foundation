
IntentBridge 7.9.3 Stabilization & 7.9.4 Initialization Summary
================================================================

Session Overview
----------------
This session represented a major architectural stabilization phase for the IntentBridge project.
The work focused primarily on:
- startup continuity compression
- locality-aware topology stabilization
- deterministic startup architecture
- replay reduction
- retrieval separation
- tooling locality
- operational readability
- IntentBridge identity migration
- bounded continuity hydration

A key realization throughout the session was that the project had evolved large amounts of replay-heavy continuity and startup inflation over previous cycles. The session intentionally shifted the architecture toward minimal deterministic operational reconstruction instead of maximal continuity replay.

Major Architectural Direction
-----------------------------
The session established the principle that startup continuity and retrieval continuity should be separated.

The new startup philosophy became:
- load only operationally necessary continuity
- avoid deep replay during startup
- hydrate continuity only when required
- preserve historical continuity through retrieval instead of automatic startup hydration

This resulted in a canonical startup pathway:

1. Governance Core
2. Topology Core
3. Active Operational State
4. Stop

Everything else becomes conditional retrieval.
