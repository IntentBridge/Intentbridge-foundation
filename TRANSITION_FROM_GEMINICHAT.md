# Transition From GeminiChat to IntentBridge

## Purpose

This document preserves continuity lineage across the project's semantic identity transition.

The project originally evolved under the working identity:

GeminiChat

Over time, the project increasingly converged toward a broader architectural and philosophical direction centered around:

- continuity-aware cognition
- alignment persistence
- human/AI collaboration
- deterministic operational continuity
- topology-aware reconstruction
- long-duration conceptual evolution

As the project matured, the original naming no longer accurately reflected the operational direction of the system.

The project therefore transitioned toward the identity:

IntentBridge

## Important Clarification

This transition is not intended to erase prior lineage.

GeminiChat represents a legitimate exploratory developmental phase in the evolution of the project.

Historical continuity, prior handoffs, earlier topology experiments, and developmental artifacts remain important parts of the architectural lineage.

IntentBridge should therefore be understood as:

The converged operational and philosophical realization emerging from the earlier exploratory GeminiChat phase.

## Continuity Philosophy

The project intentionally preserves:

- git lineage
- architectural history
- developmental evolution
- conceptual transitions
- operational continuity

The objective is not historical rewriting.

The objective is continuity-preserved evolution.

## Forward Naming Convention

Following Tier 7.8 closure and 7.9.x stabilization:

- forward operational systems
- future architectural evolution
- public repository identity
- continuity topology
- future handoff systems

will increasingly standardize around:

IntentBridge

while preserving earlier developmental lineage where appropriate.
