# Continuity Principles

## Purpose

This document explores the role of continuity within long-duration human and AI collaboration.

The objective is not perfect reconstruction.

The objective is sufficiently aligned reconstruction that preserves:

* conceptual coherence
* architectural intent
* alignment persistence
* long-term developmental continuity

## Core Ideas

* Continuity is architectural.
* Initialization quality affects reconstruction fidelity.
* Human intent should remain reconstructable over time.
* Philosophical evolution should remain historically traceable.
* Truthful exploration is more valuable than artificial certainty.

## Current Direction

IntentBridge is exploring systems and structures that preserve continuity across evolving human/AI interaction over extended developmental cycles.
